# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 21, 2026

Cyclope is a local Mac utility designed to provide menu bar controls for sleep prevention, window snapping, and custom shortcuts.

## Data Collection

Cyclope does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Cyclope does not use accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, cloud sync, or a Cyclope-hosted backend.

## Local Data

Cyclope may store app settings locally on your device, such as menu preferences, shortcut settings, sleep prevention settings, and window snapping preferences.

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party.

## Permissions

Cyclope may request macOS Accessibility permission to support features such as window snapping and global shortcuts.

This permission is used only to perform the features you enable on your Mac.

Cyclope does not use this permission to collect personal information, monitor your activity, or transmit data.

## Third Parties

Cyclope does not share data with third-party services because Cyclope does not collect user data.

## Data Retention

Any settings saved by Cyclope remain on your device until you change them, reset them, or delete the app.

## Children's Privacy

Cyclope does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
